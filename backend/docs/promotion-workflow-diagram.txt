KEY PRINCIPLES DEMONSTRATED:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. NO CURATOR ROLES: No single authority makes promotion decisions
2. OBJECTIVE CRITERIA: Mathematical thresholds, not subjective judgment
3. TRANSPARENT PROCESS: Real-time visibility of all requirements
4. EQUAL PARTICIPATION: All users start with equal weight
5. EVIDENCE-BASED WEIGHTING: Influence earned through quality contributions
6. AI GUIDANCE ONLY: AI suggests but never decides
7. AUTOMATIC PROMOTION: System executes when criteria met
8. COMMUNITY OWNERSHIP: Consensus drives truth validation
9. ANTI-GAMING PROTECTION: Multiple layers prevent manipulation
10. AUDIT TRAIL: Complete transparency and accountability
